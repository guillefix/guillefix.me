Greatest common divisor

cosmos 10th April 2019 at 9:58am
Number theory

aka greatest common factor (gcf), highest common factor (hcf), greatest common measure (gcm), or highest common divisor.

The greatest common divisor (denoted gcd)of Integers aaa and bbb is the largest positive integer that divides each of the integers, i.e. the largest ccc such that

there exists an nnn such that cn=acn = a cn=a

and

there exists an mmm such that cm=bcm = b cm=b
