Often modelled by Continuous dynamical system, but sometimes by Discrete dynamical systems, like the Logistic map

Fundamental processes

Processes that affect number of individuals in a population.

  • Death rate
  • Birth rate
  • Immigration rate
  • Emigration rate

These processes show density dependence, that is dependence on the population size itself.
