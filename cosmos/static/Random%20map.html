Random map

guillefix 17th July 2016 at 11:26pm

aka random map model, or random mapping

For each point in phase space, one chooses at random another point in phase space as being its successor in time, i.e. we have a random map, TTT, from a finite Set of MMM points, to itself. It can be shown to be a limiting case of a Kauffman Random Boolean network, with in-degree K→∞K \rightarrow \inftyK→∞.

To each attractor (labelled by sss), we assign a weight WsW_sW​s​​ corresponding to the fraction of points in its basin of attraction.
