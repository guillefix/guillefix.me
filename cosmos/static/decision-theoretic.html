Referring to Decision theory:

The mathematical study of strategies for optimal decision-making between options involving different risks or expectations of gain or loss depending on the outcome.

https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Decision_theory

See minimax_and_bayes_estimator.pdf for a description of the formalism decision theory under uncertainty (using the framework Parametric statistical inference as the setting). This also defines the useful concepts of a

  • Minimax decision rule
  • Bayes optimal decision rule
  • Admissible decision rule (related to Pareto optimality)
