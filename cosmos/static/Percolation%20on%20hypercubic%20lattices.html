Percolation on hypercubic lattices

guillefix 4th November 2016 at 1:43pm

Percolation on hypercubic lattices, which can be represented as Zd\mathbb{Z}^dZ​d​​, where ddd is the dimension of the lattice, and Z\mathbb{Z}Z is the set of integers of course.

Some mathematical results exist for Percolation thresholds, and Continuity of percolation phase transition. In particular, it is known that the percolation threshold at dimension ddd is greater than or equal that of dimension d+1d+1d+1 for percolation on Zd\mathbb{Z}^dZ​d​​.
