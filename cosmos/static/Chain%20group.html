Simplicial homology

aka group of n-chains

Definition. The Free Abelian group generated by the simplices of a Delta-complex on XXX, denoted Δn(X):=Z⟨{eαn}⟩\Delta_n(X) := \mathbb{Z} \langle\{e^n_\alpha\}\rangleΔ​n​​(X):=Z⟨{e​α​n​​}⟩. An element of the group is called an n-chain

One can define it as the free Abelian group generated by other sets of simplices, like all singular simplices, for Singular homology.
