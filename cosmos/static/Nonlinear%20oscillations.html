  • Method of Multiple Scales. We allow dependent variable xxx depend on the independent variable ttt via terms of all orders i.e. ttt, ϵt\epsilon tϵt, ϵ2t\epsilon^2 tϵ​2​​t, etc., without any constraint. A consequence is that we can treat these as independent variables, or scales. "Constants" of integration can now depend on them (i.e. on the slower scales than the scale corresponding to the order considered)
