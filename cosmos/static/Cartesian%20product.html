Cartesian product

cosmos 18th December 2018 at 9:18pm

An operation between Sets that gives a new set composed of Tuples of elements from the original sets.

For instance A×BA \times BA×B is the set of all pairs of elements, one from AAA and one from BBB. A×B×CA \times B \times CA×B×C is the set of all triplets of elements, one from AAA, the second from BBB, and the third from CCC. And so on.
