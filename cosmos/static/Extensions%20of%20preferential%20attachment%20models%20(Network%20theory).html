Models with extra edge addition

Model can consist of the BA model, but with an extra process carried out at each step. A given number of edges www is added to the network between two nodes with a probability proportional to their degree. One can again construct a master equation, and get a power law degree distribution.

Similar models exist that generalize the Price's model instead of BA.
