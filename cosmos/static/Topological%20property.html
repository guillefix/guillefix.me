Topology

aka topological invariant

Properties of Topological spaces which are invariant under Homeomorphisms

  • Cardinal functions (Cardinality)
  • Separation axiom
    • T1
    • Hausdorff
  • Connectedness
  • Countability axiom
    • First countable
    • Second countable
  • Compactness
  • Metrizability
  • Fundamental group (invariant for path-connected)
  • Homotopy group – Cohomotopy group
  • Homology and cohomology..
  • Alexandrov space.

Invariant is used more for algebraic properties, while property more for purely topological ones.
