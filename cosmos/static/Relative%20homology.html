Definition of relative homology, denoted Hn(X,A)H_n(X,A)H​n​​(X,A) See also here. See here. Defined on relative chain groups, for which we need to give the subset AAA a Singular complex (or other complex) structure, and maybe change XXX's structure to make AAA's complex a subcomplex, and the Chain group of AAA be a Subgroup of the chain group of XXX, so that we can define their quotient. Note that different complexes on the same space give the same homology, so this arbitrary choice doesn't affect the answer, and relative homology is thus well-defined.
