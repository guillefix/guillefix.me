Ruled surface

video

A surface such that for every point there is a straight line passing through it. The family of lines are called a ruling of the surface. The points used as initial points in some parametrization are called directrix.

  • Plane (surface)
  • Cylinder
  • Cone
  • Hyperboloid of revolution (1-sheet)
  • Tangent surface to a curve
  • Helicoid

The local parametrizations used are in general not parametric..
