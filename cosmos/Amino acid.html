An Organic compound with an amino group bound to a Carbon atom (known as α\alphaα), which is itself bound to a Hydrogen, and a Carboxyl group (making it an acid). The α\alphaα carbon is also bound to a side chain.

Categories of biological amino acids

Charge refers to the charge at neutral pH. They have a 3 letter code to identify them

Polar; negatively charged

Polar; positively charged

Non-polar

Hydrophobic

Special
