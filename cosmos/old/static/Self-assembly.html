Self-assembly

There are two main types:

  • Equilibrium self-assembly
  • Non-equilibrium self-assembly:
    • Steady state-non equilibrium, for dissipative systems that consume energy.
    • Transient non-equilibrium, which can be effectively stationary by virtue of a very large relaxation time-scale (like in glasses).
